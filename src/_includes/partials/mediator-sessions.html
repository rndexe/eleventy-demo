Want to become a mediator for our next exhibition? Sign up here

Mediators are integral to Science Gallery Bengaluru’s public engagement programme: our target audience of young adults not only visits and learns from, but also drives the experience on the exhibition floor and screen.

Mediator-led sessions provide the opportunity for each visitor to bring their unique insight and experiences into a conversation sparked by our exhibits, events, and learning programmes. Mediators engage and provoke questions, trigger debates, tell stories, listen, and facilitate experiences through the exhibition season.

Our Mediators are young adults between the ages of 18-29 years. Sessions are available in English, Kannada and may be offered in other Indian languages, where possible. Sign up for Mediator-led sessions here
