Activity Handbook

We observe, define, and spread contagion. Comprehending the vastness of invisible networks and transmissions is perplexing. We invite you to delve into the world of contagion through hands-on activities, experiments, and games!

This activity handbook is an accompaniment to our digital exhibition CONTAGION, and we hope that through these activities, Do-It-Yourself experiments, and puzzles, you will gain a deeper appreciation for the ways in which contagion weaves in and out of human life.

Please feel free to download the handbook, use and share it. Ideal for those who are 18 and above. Parental guidance is advised for visitors under 18 years of age.

You can share your creations with us on social media and tag us @scigalleryblr along with using the hashtags #ExperimentWithSGB and #TransmissionStartsNow.
