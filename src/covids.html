CO-VIDS

How do you connect with people while being physically distant? How are trust and emotions used and manipulated in crises? The COVID-19 pandemic has compelled us to ask many such questions ranging from the simple to the profound. If you have many questions about all the information around you, come and explore Science Gallery Bengaluru’s Co-Vids series.

In this series of three-minute videos, experts from disciplines such as medicine, epidemiology, public policy, history, and psychiatry draw on their deep experience of thinking about contagions to pose—and explore—the top three questions on their minds.
